## Angle 3 — Meta-Analysis as a Distinct Methodological Strand

- `"meta-analysis" "sport" OR "exercise" heterogeneity methods`

## Angle 5 — Scoping Reviews and Rapid Reviews as Alternatives

Scoping review methodology has become a distinct formal tradition (Arksey and O'Malley; JBI framework), often used when questions are too broad for a conventional SLR. Searching "systematic review" alone misses this adjacent literature.

- `"scoping review" "sport science" OR "exercise science" methodology`

## Angle 6 — Publication Bias and Small-Study Effects in Sports Research

Sports science faces acute publication bias problems (small labs, strong null-aversion, pressure on effect sizes). The failure-case literature on this exists partly outside the standard SLR methods canon and is found under replication/reproducibility framing.

- `"publication bias" "sports science" OR "exercise science" systematic review`
- `"replication crisis" "sport science" statistical power underpowered`
