# Kit-Maker Grounding Map

Tracks every significant design decision against the research bar the kit sets for itself: medium+ confidence empirical, adversarially verified. Consensus and anecdote are flagged; heuristics with no angle assigned are the highest-risk entries.

**Status key:**
- ✅ VALIDATED — synthesis ran AND produced a clear result from medium+ confidence empirical claims AND that result was applied to the kit. This is the only status that means "we actually know this."
- 🔬 SYNTHESIZED — synthesis ran, but the result was contested, insufficient, or consensus-only. Decision remains provisional. Does NOT mean validated.
- 📋 ARCHIVE EXISTS — research ran and archive files were written. Evidence quality unknown until synthesis. Do NOT treat as meaningful backing — an archive full of low-confidence or null-result claims is no better than unresearched.
- 🔶 RESEARCH QUEUED — angle assigned in RESEARCH_ANGLES.md, not yet run
- ⚠️ CONSENSUS ONLY — practitioner consensus, no empirical literature available for this topic; explicitly accepted as the best available evidence given the field
- ❌ UNRESEARCHED — no angle assigned, pure heuristic or design intuition; highest-risk status
- 🏳️ CLOSED — N angles tried, result was consistently null/consensus/insufficient; accepted as best-available; will not pursue further unless the reopen trigger fires. See Closed Decisions section for the full record.

**Trials column:** number of distinct research angles that have been run specifically targeting this decision. 0 = never tried. Each synthesis that returns null/consensus increments the count. A decision can be closed after 2+ trials with consistent null/consensus results and an explicit closure decision.

**The trap this file exists to prevent:** marking something 📋 ARCHIVE EXISTS and treating it as if the decision is backed by research. It is not. Archive existence means "we did the legwork," not "we have a result."

A decision stays provisional until ✅ VALIDATED. Everything else — including 🔬 SYNTHESIZED — may still be wrong.

**Closure policy:** close (🏳️) when: (a) 2+ targeted angles returned consistent null or consensus results, AND (b) a deliberate decision was made to accept current wording as best-available. Reopen trigger must be specific: not "if new research appears" but "if a controlled study with n>20 on [specific topic] is published or found."
