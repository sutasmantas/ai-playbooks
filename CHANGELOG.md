## [0.1.0] - 2026-07-08

### Added

- **angle-gen-kit** — research angle generation playbook, validated across 22 controlled trial inputs
  - Structural completeness: Q7 avg 3.0/3 (vocabulary novelty — each angle uses non-overlapping language)
  - Formulation quality: Q5 avg 2.82/3 (framing distinctiveness — each angle takes a distinct epistemic approach)
  - Query precision: Q3 avg 2.73/3
  - Procedural compliance: 100% BIN1/BIN2 pass rate across all 22 inputs
  - Longitudinal quality stability: A8 = 3 across 10 sequential inputs
- Research archive: 12 research files covering vocabulary diversity, angle generation methodology, and adversarial quality checks
- Weakness register and strength register documenting known edge cases and validated behaviors
