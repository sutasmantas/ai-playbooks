Day 1

Drive down + Kraków evening

Leave Kaunas early — about 7 hours on the S61/S7 via Warsaw, free expressway the whole way. Roll into Kraków mid-afternoon and let the Old Town do the rest.

  • Stretch stops: Suwałki, an Augustów lake swim, Łomża food court.
  • Evening loop: Old Town & Cloth Hall → Wawel at golden hour → walk to Kazimierz.
  • Dinner: zapiekanka at the Plac Nowy kiosks, then Kazimierz bars for drinks.
Day 2

Energylandia → the mountains

At the gate for 10:00 — that one decision protects the whole day. Sprint the back of the park first, eat off-peak, ride the water coasters when it's hot, then drive to the Night-2 base.

  • Ride order: Zadra → Hyperion → Abyssus → Formuła → Speed Water Coaster.
  • If it's hot: Aqualantis water park is included — bring swimwear.
  • Evening: ~2h scenic drive to Zakopane, highlander dinner.
Day 3

Tatra Mountains

Dawn start — June afternoons bring thunderstorms, so be high early. Hike Morskie Oko and push past the lake to Czarny Staw for the views the day-trippers skip. Recover in the evening.

  • The hike: ~8 km each way on tarmac — skip the pony carts, they only cover 1.5 km.
  • The payoff: +2.2 km up to Czarny Staw pod Rysami, fewer people, best views.
  • Evening: thermal baths at Bukovina, then a karczma dinner — oscypek & kwaśnica.
Day 4 optional

Salt mine + drive home

Bochnia or Wieliczka in the morning, then the long haul north. If everyone's spent, split it with a Warsaw overnight rather than doing it in one shot after the mountains.

  • Our pick: Bochnia — underground slide + salt-lake boat, fewer crowds, cheaper.
  • Beat the traffic: leave before the Zakopianka clogs on the way out.
  • Or skip it: the 3-day version just drives home relaxed on the morning of Day 4.

Good to know

The stuff that trips people up.

01Carry passports. LT–PL border checks are on until Oct 2026 — keep them reachable, not in the boot.
02Driver stays dry. Poland's alcohol limit is effectively zero — designated driver, not "one beer."
03Fuel at hypermarkets. E.Leclerc / Auchan / Carrefour stations are cheapest; avoid motorway pumps.
04Beat the Zakopianka. Leave Kraków before ~07:00 — weekend jams on that road run for hours.

🪪 Documents · 🚗 Car & driving laws

Legal, insured, road-ready.

  • Passports — carry on you. LT–PL border spot-checks run to Oct 2026; keep them in the cabin, not the boot.
  • EHIC + travel insurance + breakdown cover. Confirm the policy covers Poland & Slovakia (if you do the Slovak day) and mountain rescue.
  • All 3 drivers named on the insurance and carrying their licence — check this before leaving, not at a swap.
  • Mandatory in-car kit (Poland): a warning triangle and a fire extinguisher are required; a hi-vis vest for the driver is strongly advised (one per person is best). Carry the registration + insurance proof.
  • Service check before you go: tyres (tread + pressure, incl. spare), oil, coolant, washer fluid, wipers, lights, brakes. June = summer tyres are fine.
  • Headlights on day & night, all year — it's the law in Poland.
  • Alcohol limit is 0.2‰ — effectively zero. The designated driver doesn't drink at all; penalties are heavy.
  • Average-speed (section) cameras on the S7 — they clock your average over a stretch, so don't sprint between them. The Yanosik app warns you.
  • Kraków Clean Transport Zone (LEZ): central Kraków restricts older, high-emission cars. Before the trip, check your car's Euro emission class (on the V5/registration doc) against the current Kraków rules — and if in doubt, just don't drive into the core: park at a Park & Ride and take a tram. A foreign car may still need a registration/sticker, so verify on the city site near the date.

🅿️ Tolls & parking · 🆘 Safety

Park smart, stay safe.

  • A4 toll Kraków–Katowice — small cash/Autopay.
  • Kraków — Park & Ride or a guarded lot; don't drive the Old Town.
  • Zakopane — paid parking (often 7-day passes).
  • Morskie Oko — pre-booked TPN e-ticket or the Bar Fis minibus (no cash on site).
  • Emergencies: 112 · mountain rescue TOPR 601 100 300.
  • Watch pickpockets (Main Square/trams) & car break-ins — nothing on show.
