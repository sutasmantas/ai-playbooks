Energylandia — Poland's #1 theme park with 20+ world-class coasters 4.9

Coaster Priority Order (to beat queues):

  • Hyperion (opens sharp at 10, queues explode by 10:30)

  • Zadra (Dragon Zone — go straight after Hyperion)

  • Abyssus (Extreme Zone)

  • Formuła → Speed Water Coaster (afternoon, when heat builds)

Tips:

  • Energy Pass (~219 zł) gives skip-the-line access to top coasters — worth it on busy days

  • Pack a dry bag or change of clothes for water rides

  • Bring snacks/water from the car — park food is pricey

Day 2 Food Options (Zakopane evening):

  • Chata Zbójnicka 4.5 — classic highlander food, wood-fire atmosphere, PLN 40–60

Estimated Total per Person (~3 friends splitting costs):

  • Fuel (split 3 ways, ~1,440 km total): ~€35–45/person (depends on car)

  • Energylandia parking: 30 zł (~€7, split)

  • Kasprowy Wierch cable car: ~70 zł (~€16)

Variable Costs (Estimates)

  • Kraków apartment (split 3): ~€20–40/person/night

  • Zakopane guesthouse (split 3): ~€15–25/person/night

  • Food (3 days): ~€30–50/person

  • Estimated total: ~€160–230/person (very solid for 3 days!)

PACKING LIST

Road Trip Essentials

  • Car documents, green card insurance, passport

  • Vignette / road tolls — Poland has no highway vignette (free motorways )

  • Phone mount + charger cable

  • Snacks & drinks for the drive (save money!)

  • Reusable water bottle

Energylandia Day

  • Change of clothes / dry bag (water rides!)

  • Comfortable sneakers — you'll walk 10+ km

  • Sunscreen & sunglasses

  • Small backpack (lockers available but cost extra)

  • Cash (PLN) — some stands are cash only

Mountains (Day 3)

  • Hiking boots or trail shoes

  • Light waterproof jacket (mountain weather changes fast)

  • Layers — Tatra peaks can be cold even in summer

  • Snacks for the hike
