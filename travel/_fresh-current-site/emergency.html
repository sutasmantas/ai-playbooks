On the road · after dark

🚗 The drive home

Tired driver → swap, or use the emergency nearby stay. Never push a sleepy ~2 h drive.😴
Wildlife on rural roads at night — slow down, full beams between cars.🦌
Breakdown → triangle + hi-vis vest; call your roadside number.⚠️
Headlights required day & night in Lithuania.💡
Weather cancels

☁️ Night plan-B

  1. Call cancelled by ~14:00 → decide as a crew: reschedule the night, or go anyway for the day.
  2. Go-anyway: day tour / observation deck / free outdoor exhibition / Mindūnai tower / Molėtai food.
  3. Rain or wind → skip tower & lake stops; museum indoors + a warm meal.
  4. Reschedule the telescope via the museum (number above).

Documents

🛂 Lost passport / ID

  1. Report it to local police (112 / 997) — get a written report.
  2. Contact the Lithuanian Embassy in Warsaw for an emergency travel document.
  3. Use your saved digital + paper photocopy of the passport to speed it up.
  4. At the LT–PL border, the emergency document + police report get you home.
