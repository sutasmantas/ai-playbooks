## POLAND — page 3: THE PLAN (final itinerary)

**Summary:** *Kaunas → Kraków → Energylandia (Zator) → Tatra Mountains → home. Up to 4 days, 3 friends, lower-budget, late June 2026.*

**Day 1 — Drive down + Kraków evening**
Leave Kaunas early; ~7 h on the S61/S7 via Warsaw (stretch stops: Suwałki, Augustów lake swim, Łomża). Evening in Kraków: Old Town, Wawel at golden hour, Kazimierz dinner (zapiekanka at Plac Nowy), drinks.

**Day 2 — Energylandia (full thrill day) → mountains**
At the gate for 10:00. Ride order: Hyperion → Zadra → Abyssus → Formuła/Speed Water Coaster; Aqualantis if hot. Evening drive to Zakopane.

**Day 3 — Tatra Mountains (nature/adventure)**
Dawn start. Morskie Oko hike (push on to Czarny Staw for the best views). Evening thermal baths + a highlander *karczma* dinner (oscypek, kwaśnica).

**Day 4 (optional) — Salt mine + drive home**
Bochnia or Wieliczka in the morning, then the long drive back (split with a Warsaw overnight if tired).

**Where we stay (vetted, free-cancellation):**
- Kraków: *Greg & Tom Hostel* (top-rated, free breakfast+dinner) or *Apartment-Kazimierz w/ parking*.
- Zakopane: *Hostel Stara Polana* (by the Morskie Oko bus) or *Hostel Wielka Krokiew* (cheapest, free parking).

**Book ahead (sells out):** Energylandia e-tickets (+ pass), Morskie Oko parking e-ticket *or* the minibus from Bar Fis, salt-mine tour slot, all 3 nights' lodging.

**Budget:** ≈ **€285/person** core; ≈ **€340** with the Energy Pass; ≈ **€250** for the 3-day version.

**Good-to-know:** carry passports (LT–PL border checks on until Oct 2026); Polish alcohol limit ~0 for the driver; fuel cheapest at hypermarkets; leave Kraków before ~07:00 to beat the Zakopianka traffic.
