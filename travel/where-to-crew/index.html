How it works

Three pages per destination. Sign up, draft, lock it in.

01 / Sign up

Say you're in

Name, dates you're free, stops you'd pin on the map, your vibe and budget. It feeds the draft — real votes, not guesses.

02 / Plan draft

Build it together

Day-by-day choices, each showing its cost, its time, and what it squeezes. A live per-person total and a feasibility meter keep everyone honest.

03 / Plan

Print and go

The draft becomes a deep, day-by-day plan — timeline, bookings, packing, Plan-B, an emergency one-pager — ready to download.
