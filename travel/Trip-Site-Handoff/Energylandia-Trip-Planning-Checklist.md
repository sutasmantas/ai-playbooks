## 6. Tolls, Parking & Navigation

- [ ] **No vignette needed** — LT and PL have no car vignette; the S61/S8/S7 route is **toll-free**. Only the **A4 west of Kraków** is tolled (18 PLN/gate at Balice + Mysłowice) — you may hit it only on the Energylandia leg
- [ ] Pay tolls / city parking by app: **Autopay** or **SkyCash** (also A4 pay-by-plate)
- [ ] Energylandia **parking 30 PLN/day** — buy with tickets online (cheaper) or on-site
- [ ] **Kraków**: never drive to the Old Town (pedestrian core). Full per-stop parking detail in **🅿️ Where to park** (Route Map section below)
- [ ] Offline maps downloaded (Google Maps / Maps.me) for PL route + Kraków + Tatras
- [ ] Route shared in group chat as backup

### 🚓 Polish driving laws — don't get fined
- [ ] **Alcohol limit 0.02%** (effectively zero) — designated driver stays **fully dry**, not "one beer"
- [ ] **Section speed cameras** ("odcinkowy pomiar") measure your *average* over a stretch — hold the limit the whole way; braking at the camera won't help. Known zones: **S7 near Radom** (Kopana, Falęcice, Kępiny — all 120) and the **Zakopianka tunnel (90)**
- [ ] Speed limits: **140** (motorway A) · **120** (expressway S) · **50** in towns
- [ ] Install **Yanosik** (Polish Waze) for speed-camera / section / police alerts
- [ ] Foreign-plate cars get **on-the-spot fines** (card accepted) — keep registration + insurance handy

## 🗺️ Detailed Route Map & Stops

> **Kaunas → S61 Via Baltica → Warsaw → S7 → Kraków → Zakopianka → Zakopane.** Mostly free expressway. Pure driving ≈ **6.5–7.5 h to Kraków** + ~2 h to Zakopane (much more if it jams — see Leg C). Break every ~2–2.5 h.

> ⚠️ **Sunday trading ban:** most malls/supermarkets are **closed Sundays** in Poland — 2026 has only 8 trading Sundays, incl. **Jun 28 = OPEN**, but **Jun 21 = CLOSED**. On a closed Sunday, mall **food courts still serve**, and **Żabka shops / fuel stations / MOP rest areas / restaurants** stay open. Check your travel date.

### Leg A — Kaunas → Warsaw (~430 km · ~4.5 h driving / ~5.5–6 h w/ stops)
| ~Time in | Stop | What's there | Verdict |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1:00 | **Border** Kalvarija→Budzisko | Spot-checks on until Oct 2026; ~15 min, peaks 60–90 | Cross weekday mid-morning; passports out |
| 1:45 | **Suwałki — Suwałki Plaza** | Food court, 455 free spaces (350 underground), toilets | ⭐ Best first PL break |
| 2:10 | **Augustów — Lake Necko** | Quick swim/stretch, town beach, free parking | ⭐ Best warm-weather reset |
| 3:40 | **Łomża — Galeria Veneda** | KFC/café food court, Carrefour, 580 free spaces | ⭐ Best-timed 2nd break |
| 4:00+ | *Ostrołęka — Galeria Bursztynowa* | Kaufland, 816 free spaces — but ~30 km off route | Only if you need it |

- Fuel: prices ≈ equal LT/PL — but Reddit says **cheapest = hypermarket stations (E.Leclerc / Auchan / Carrefour)**; avoid motorway/Orlen-highway stations. Top up PL-side near Suwałki/Augustów. MOPs Żubryn/Budzisko, Górki, Niekrasy on the S61.
- Optional detour: **Wigry Monastery** (lakeside, ~1.5 h round) · **Tykocin** (baroque town) — only if you've built in time.

### Leg B — Warsaw → Kraków (~290 km · ~3 h · S7 = toll-free)
> S7 now bypasses the cities, so each mall is a 3–8 min detour off the expressway.

| ~Time in | Stop | What's there | Verdict |
|---|---|---|---|
| Warsaw exit | **Galeria Mokotów** (south side) | Big food court, 2,600 spaces (2 h free) | Good if leaving Warsaw S-side |
| 1:15 | *Radom — Galeria Słoneczna / M1* | Food court + Carrefour; M1 = faster toilets+food | Only if early break needed |
| 2:15 | **Kielce — Galeria Echo / Korona** | Big food court, ~2,100 free covered spaces | ⭐ Main Leg-B break |
| 3:00 | **S Kraków — Bonarka City Center** | 270 shops, 30+ eateries, 3,200 FREE spaces | ⭐ Eat/fuel/stage before the mountains |

- Optional: **Kielce** Bishops' Palace + old town (~1 h) for a real sightseeing pause.

### Leg C — Kraków → Zakopane (~100 km · ~2 h… or 3–7 h in jams)
> ⚠️ **Zakopianka traffic** — the classic bottleneck. Jams hit **Fri afternoon / Sat morning south-bound** and **Sun afternoon/evening north-bound (your return!)**.
> **Real slow section:** **Rabka → Nowy Targ** opened Aug 2024 (new 2-lane road, 100 km/h) — that part's fine now. But **Nowy Targ → Zakopane stays old single-carriageway** (upgrade not due until ~2028): overtaking risk + the town-entrance crawl. The tunnel has a **90 km/h section camera**. S7's **Mistrzejowice** node near Kraków may still be a single-lane pinch in June 2026. No real shortcut exists — **timing beats routing**.

- [ ] **Time it:** leave Kraków **before ~07:00** or in the evening; avoid Fri/Sun 14:00–20:00
- [ ] **Last reset before Zakopane:** **Nowy Targ** supermarkets (Biedronka/Lidl/Kaufland) — no food-court mall yet (being built); clean toilets, cheap food, free parking
- [ ] Fuel up in Kraków (Bonarka) — Zakopianka stations queue on jam days
- Free photo stop: **Chochołów wooden village** (just west of the direct line — better as a side-trip from Zakopane, Termy next door)

### Return leg (Zakopane/Kraków → Kaunas — ~11 h, split it)
> Don't do it in one shot after the mountains. Best split: **Day 1 → Warsaw** (clears the worst roads + Zakopianka early), **Day 2 Warsaw → Kaunas** on the clean S61, timing Budzisko late-morning. Alt: overnight in **Suwałki** to hit the border first thing.

### 🅿️ Where to park the car (per stop)
- [ ] **Warsaw (short stop):** street parking (SPPN) is **free weekends & evenings** (paid Mon–Fri 08–20, ~16 PLN/3 h). Weekday → edge-of-centre garage (~6–10 PLN/h) + walk in. Never drive to the Old Town.
- [ ] **Kraków (overnight):** Kazimierz is in the **paid zone** (Mon–Sat 10–20; **free Sun + 20:00–10:00**) and its Plac Nowy core is restricted. Cheapest: **P+R at a tram terminus (Czerwone Maki / Bieżanów) ~10 PLN/day for the whole car**; easiest: a **guarded Kazimierz lot ~50 PLN/night**. An LTZ permit does NOT waive the parking fee.
- [ ] **Energylandia:** gate car park **~30 PLN/day**, arrive **~09:30**; pre-book entry tickets online to skip the queue.
- [ ] **Zakopane:** street parking is now **paid 7 days/week incl. weekends, 08:00–21:00** (changed Dec 2025; ~6–7 PLN first hr). Use a guarded lot off Krupówki (~10–40 PLN/day, June = high end); **Kuźnice** (Kasprowy) fills early — arrive early.
- [ ] **Morskie Oko:** TPN car park is **e-ticket prebook only** (~70–75 PLN peak, no on-gate cash) — or skip it and take the **minibus from Bar Fis, ul. Kościuszki 22 (~10–15 PLN, leaves when full)**.
- [ ] **Paying:** SkyCash / moBiLET / mPay accept **foreign plates**; pay by card / Apple-Google Pay (not BLIK). Meters take contactless as a no-app fallback (often no change given).

## 💬 Reddit Wisdom (real-traveller tips)

> Honest, on-the-ground tips mined from Reddit + travel forums (r/poland, r/krakow, r/lithuania, r/rollercoasters, Tatra forums). These are **opinions/experiences, not verified facts** — Reddit was partly crawler-blocked, so some come from indexed snippets + corroborating forums. Trust the consensus ones; double-check anything load-bearing.

**🚗 Driving & border**
- [ ] **At the border, just drive straight** — Google Maps routes you onto a bad detour right after the crossing; **use Waze**, ignore Google's detours near the border.
- [ ] **Foreign-plate fines = pay on the spot** (cash or card — they carry terminals); nothing gets mailed home. Police use **mobile radar in villages**, not just fixed cameras. Carry both cash and a card.
- [ ] **Cheapest fuel = hypermarket stations** (E.Leclerc / Auchan / Carrefour); **avoid motorway/Orlen-highway stations** (priciest).
- [ ] **Don't hog the left lane** (fineable), **no right turn on red ever**, pedestrians have priority, and **Warsaw/Kraków drivers are aggressive** — drive defensively, don't get baited.
- [ ] Foreign cars are judged by **home-country equipment rules** (an LT-legal car is legal in PL), but still carry the triangle + vest.
- [ ] **Zakopianka:** leave Kraków **4–5 AM** to beat it — weekend jams hit 3–5 h. (Reddit strongly seconds the route-map advice.)

**🎢 Energylandia**
- [ ] **Lockers:** ~15 PLN wristband = all-day unlimited (one cabinet at a time) — grab it **before** gates open.
- [ ] **You can leave & re-enter** to eat cheaper nearby, and outside food/water is allowed in. Real budget win for 3.
- [ ] **Tue–Thu are lightest;** on a quiet weekday max queues can be ~20 min and one day clears all the majors.
- [ ] ⚠️ **Before buying the paid pass:** older threads mention a *free* per-ride "Fast Pass" (scan your ticket for one timed return per big coaster), but newer info says it went paid in 2024. **Verify the current 2026 product** on the official site so you don't over-pay (or assume free when it isn't).
- [ ] Expectations: enthusiasts love **Hyperion & Zadra**; a few find **Abyssus** underwhelming vs the hype.

**🍺 Kraków**
- [ ] **Exchange cash on ul. Sławkowska** (good rates) — avoid kantors on Rynek/Grodzka/in malls; **"0% commission" ≠ good rate** (check the buy/sell spread). Skip airport exchange.
- [ ] Cheap eats: **Okrąglak** zapiekanka (Plac Nowy), counter-service **Przystanek Pierogarnia**, any **bar mleczny**.
- [ ] Nightlife: **Pijalnia Wódki i Piwa** (kitsch dive, ~5–8 PLN shots) + **Alchemia** on Plac Nowy; drink in Kazimierz, don't get funnelled into Old Town clubs.
- [ ] **Walk past anyone inviting you to a club / "cabaret"** — the tout scam ("pay once, drink all night") ends in spiked drinks + emptied cards (real arrests in 2023).

**🏔️ Tatras / Zakopane**
- [ ] **Don't take the horse carts** to Morskie Oko — controversial on animal welfare *and* poor value (~100 PLN, still drops you ~2 km short). Walk or take the minibus.
- [ ] **Eat one street off Krupówki** (tourist trap): **Bąkowo Zohylina Niźnio**, **Góralska Tradycja** for highlander food; portions are huge — under-order.
- [ ] **Oscypek authenticity:** real sheep's-milk oscypek is summer-only and late June is borderline — ask the vendor / buy from a trail farm, not a Krupówki stall.
- [ ] **Thermal baths:** **Bukovina** = best value + mountain views for a short post-hike soak; **Chochołowskie** = biggest but most crowded.
- [ ] **Bad-weather plan B:** in fog/storm, Morskie Oko shows you fog, not the lake — switch to town + baths. June = peak afternoon-thunderstorm month, so start at dawn.
- [ ] Quieter scenic alternative for fit hikers: approach via **Dolina Pięciu Stawów (Five Lakes Valley)** — less asphalt, fewer people.
