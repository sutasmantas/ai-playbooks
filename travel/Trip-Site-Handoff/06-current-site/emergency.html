Documents

🛂 Lost passport / ID

  1. Report it to local police (112 / 997) — get a written report.
  2. Contact the Lithuanian Embassy in Warsaw for an emergency travel document.
  3. Use your saved digital + paper photocopy of the passport to speed it up.
  4. At the LT–PL border, the emergency document + police report get you home.
