# Option D: No-Recipe Single-Ingredient
*A daily food list. No meals, no timing, no recipes. Eat the items in any order. Generated 2026-06-29.*

## WHY THIS VARIANT
Schoenfeld 2015 (15 RCTs) found zero body composition difference between 2-3 and 5-6 meals when calories and protein are equated, eliminating any scientific basis for meal structure. Sacks 2009 (n=811, 2-year RCT) confirmed that macro ratio does not drive fat loss when calories are held constant. These two null results mean that any diet imposing meal timing, meal composition, or eating windows is adding complexity with no return — a daily ingredient list is the minimum viable structure.
