# Deload Week — 2026-05-25

**Rules for every session:**
- Sets are cut in half vs. the main plan
- Weights stay the same — do not reduce load
- Stop 4–5 reps short of failure on every set (if you could do 6, stop at 2; if you could do 12, stop at 7)
- For new exercises (lateral raises, Y-raise, serratus punch, Nordic curl, hip adduction): use this week to learn the movement — weights are set in the tables below; focus on technique from the form notes in plan.md
- Face pulls are NOT new — use your current working weight (29+15kg bands)
- No slow negatives on pull-ups this week
