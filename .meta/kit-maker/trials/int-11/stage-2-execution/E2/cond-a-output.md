## Angle 7: Block Periodization vs Traditional Periodization

A specific sub-debate within periodization literature. Block periodization (Issurin) is proposed as advantageous for athletes with constrained recovery, which maps onto masters athletes. This vocabulary tradition rarely appears in masters-athlete searches.
