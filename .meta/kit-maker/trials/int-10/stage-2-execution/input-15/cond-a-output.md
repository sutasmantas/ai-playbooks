# Research Angle List: Systematic Literature Reviews in AI and ML Research

**Topic:** How to conduct systematic literature reviews in AI and ML research — what methods, databases, and approaches give comprehensive coverage.

---

## Angle 1: PRISMA-Based Protocol Design

**Vocabulary tradition / field:** Evidence-based medicine / Clinical epidemiology (Cochrane Collaboration)

**Search queries:**
- `"systematic literature review" "artificial intelligence" OR "machine learning" PRISMA protocol methodology`
- `PRISMA 2020 "machine learning" checklist reporting guidelines`
- `"preferred reporting items" systematic review AI research protocol registration`

**Best source type:** Academic journals (JAMIA, Journal of Clinical Epidemiology, BMJ); Cochrane Handbook; PRISMA statement website.

---

## Angle 2: Software Engineering SLR Guidelines (Kitchenham)

**Vocabulary tradition / field:** Software engineering research methodology

**Search queries:**
- `Kitchenham "systematic literature review" guidelines software engineering machine learning`
- `"guidelines for performing systematic literature reviews" Kitchenham Charters 2007 replication`
- `SLR protocol "search strategy" "inclusion exclusion criteria" software engineering AI`

**Best source type:** Academic journals (Information and Software Technology, Empirical Software Engineering); Keele University technical report (Kitchenham & Charters 2007).

- `arXiv preprint "systematic review" inclusion grey literature machine learning coverage`

## Angle 10: Scoping Reviews and Mapping Studies

- `scoping review "artificial intelligence" methodology Arksey O'Malley framework`

**Best source type:** Academic journals (BMC Medical Research Methodology, Systematic Reviews journal); JBI scoping review methodology guidance; practitioner guides from health sciences.
