# Research Angles: Chain-of-Thought Reasoning in LLMs

Topic: Chain-of-thought (CoT) reasoning in large language models — when it works, when it fails, and why.

---

## Angle 1: Prompting Methodology and Few-Shot CoT

**Vocabulary tradition:** NLP / prompting research (ML conferences: NeurIPS, ICML, ACL, EMNLP)

**Search queries:**
- `"chain-of-thought" prompting few-shot reasoning large language models`
- `"let's think step by step" zero-shot chain-of-thought LLM`
- `chain-of-thought prompting arithmetic commonsense reasoning benchmarks`

**Best source type:** Academic preprints (arXiv cs.CL, cs.AI), ACL Anthology proceedings
